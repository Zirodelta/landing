MEXC vs Bluefin

Side-by-side comparison of funding rate mechanics, fees, and perpetual trading on MEXC and Bluefin.

Verdict

MEXC suits arbitrageurs targeting altcoin perpetuals with zero maker fees and established liquidity, despite volume concerns. Bluefin offers innovative on-chain perpetuals with faster funding cycles but requires comfort with Sui ecosystem limitations and lower overall volumes.

Rate Mechanics

MEXC uses traditional 8-hour funding intervals with rates calculated from premium and interest components, often showing higher volatility due to aggressive altcoin listings. Bluefin implements hourly funding on Sui blockchain with on-chain rate calculations, providing more frequent arbitrage opportunities but requiring gas fees for position adjustments. MEXC's zero maker fees benefit funding arbitrageurs, while Bluefin's hourly intervals allow for more responsive rate corrections but higher transaction frequency.
