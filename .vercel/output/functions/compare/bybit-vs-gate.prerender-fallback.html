Bybit vs Gate.io

Side-by-side comparison of funding rate mechanics, fees, and perpetual trading on Bybit and Gate.io.

Verdict

Bybit excels for high-frequency funding arbitrage on major pairs due to superior infrastructure and liquidity. Gate.io offers better fee structures and broader token coverage for opportunistic arbitrage on emerging altcoins.

Rate Mechanics

Both exchanges calculate funding rates every 8 hours using mark price vs spot index methodology. Gate.io offers lower maker fees at 0.015% compared to Bybit's 0.02%, potentially improving arbitrage profitability. Bybit's superior matching engine and liquidity depth on major pairs typically results in tighter spreads and more predictable funding rate movements. Gate.io's extensive altcoin selection provides more arbitrage opportunities but with higher execution risk on low-volume pairs.
