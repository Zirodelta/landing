Aevo vs Bluefin

Side-by-side comparison of funding rate mechanics, fees, and perpetual trading on Aevo and Bluefin.

Verdict

Aevo offers unique advantages for traders seeking options-perps arbitrage opportunities but faces liquidity constraints. Bluefin provides a more focused perpetual trading experience with superior infrastructure but operates within the nascent Sui ecosystem with limited trading pairs.

Rate Mechanics

Both Aevo and Bluefin calculate funding rates hourly using traditional perpetual futures methodology based on the premium/discount to spot prices. Aevo's rates may be more volatile due to its smaller liquidity pools and combined options market dynamics affecting price discovery. Bluefin benefits from Sui's fast finality for rate updates but may experience wider spreads due to limited market makers. Both platforms use standard 8-hour TWAP calculations for rate determination.
