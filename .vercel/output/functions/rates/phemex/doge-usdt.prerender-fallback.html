How Funding Works on Phemex

Phemex calculates DOGE/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC, using a time-weighted average of the premium index over the funding period. The exchange applies a dampening mechanism when rates exceed ±0.75% to prevent extreme funding charges. During high volatility periods, Phemex may implement additional risk controls including position limits and margin adjustments for DOGE contracts. The funding fee is exchanged directly between long and short position holders, with no intermediary fees charged by the exchange.
