How Funding Works on Phemex

Phemex calculates ARB/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using time-weighted average premium over the funding period. The exchange caps funding rates at ±0.75% per cycle to prevent excessive volatility. Long positions pay funding when rates are positive, while short positions receive payments, with settlements occurring automatically in USDT margin accounts.
