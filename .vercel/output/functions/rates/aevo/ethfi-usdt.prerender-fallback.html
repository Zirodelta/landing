How Funding Works on Aevo

Aevo calculates funding rates hourly using the time-weighted average price difference between perpetual and mark prices over the preceding hour. The rollup architecture enables rapid funding settlements without Ethereum mainnet congestion delays. Funding payments occur automatically at the top of each hour, with long positions paying short positions when funding is positive. Aevo's mark price incorporates multiple CEX reference prices to prevent manipulation and ensure fair funding calculations.
