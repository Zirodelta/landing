Aevo TAO/USDT Funding Rate

TAO/USDT perpetual futures on Aevo offer unique funding rate arbitrage opportunities through the platform's rollup-based architecture. With hourly funding payments and moderate trading volume, TAO perps can exhibit significant rate volatility during AI market cycles.

How Funding Works on Aevo

Aevo calculates funding rates every hour based on the premium between the perpetual future price and the underlying index. The rate is determined by the 8-hour TWAP of the premium, but payment occurs hourly, creating more frequent settlement cycles. As a rollup-based DEX, Aevo's funding calculations are processed off-chain and settled on-chain, allowing for more responsive rate adjustments compared to traditional AMM-based platforms.
