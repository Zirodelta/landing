Aevo ATOM/USDT Funding Rate

ATOM/USDT perpetual futures on Aevo DEX offer unique funding rate arbitrage opportunities with 1-hour funding intervals on a dedicated rollup infrastructure. How Funding Works on Aevo

Aevo calculates funding rates every hour using a time-weighted average price mechanism between the perpetual future and spot index. The exchange's rollup architecture enables rapid funding rate updates while maintaining decentralized settlement. Funding payments are automatically debited or credited to trader positions based on the hourly rate, with long positions paying shorts when rates are positive. Aevo's off-chain order book provides tighter spreads for ATOM perpetuals compared to purely on-chain DEXs.
