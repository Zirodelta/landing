How Funding Works on Bybit

Bybit calculates RENDER/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using the Premium Index formula. The rate reflects the difference between the perpetual contract price and the underlying spot price, with a dampening mechanism to reduce volatility. Long positions pay funding when rates are positive, while shorts pay when negative, creating clear arbitrage entry points for traders.
