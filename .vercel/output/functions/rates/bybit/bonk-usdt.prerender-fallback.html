How Funding Works on Bybit

Bybit calculates BONK/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using the premium index between perpetual and spot prices. The funding rate is determined by the 8-hour time-weighted average premium with interest rate components. When funding rates are positive, long position holders pay shorts, creating arbitrage opportunities for traders who can hedge their spot BONK holdings. Bybit caps funding rates at ±2% to prevent extreme scenarios during volatile periods.
