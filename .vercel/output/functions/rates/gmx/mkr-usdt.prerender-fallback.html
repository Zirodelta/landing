How Funding Works on GMX

GMX calculates MKR/USDT funding rates using Chainlink price feeds and position imbalances within the GLP pool. Funding payments occur hourly based on the difference between perpetual and spot prices, with rates typically ranging from -0.1% to 0.1% per hour. Long positions pay funding when rates are positive, while shorts pay when negative. The oracle-based system reduces manipulation risks but creates dependency on external price feeds for accurate rate calculations.
