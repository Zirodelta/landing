GMX DOGE/USDT Funding Rate

GMX's DOGE/USDT perpetual contract operates on Arbitrum using oracle-based pricing from Chainlink feeds with hourly funding rate settlements. Unlike traditional order book exchanges, GMX uses GLP liquidity pools as counterparty, creating unique funding rate dynamics influenced by pool utilization and oracle price feeds.

How Funding Works on GMX

Funding rates on GMX are calculated hourly based on the difference between perpetual price and oracle spot price, with additional adjustments for pool skew and utilization. Long positions pay funding to shorts when rates are positive, with payments automatically deducted from collateral every hour. The oracle dependency means funding rates can diverge from pure market sentiment, as they incorporate Chainlink's aggregated spot price data rather than just perpetual trading activity.
