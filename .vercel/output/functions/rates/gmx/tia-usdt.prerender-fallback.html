How Funding Works on GMX

GMX calculates TIA/USDT funding rates every hour based on the net open interest between long and short positions. When longs dominate, they pay shorts through the funding mechanism, with rates derived from Chainlink oracle prices and position imbalances. The decentralized nature means funding rates can be more volatile than centralized exchanges, especially during periods of high open interest concentration. GMX's 0.05% maker and 0.07% taker fees apply to position changes, which must be factored into arbitrage calculations.
