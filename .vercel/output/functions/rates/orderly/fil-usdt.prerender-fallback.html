Orderly Network FIL/USDT Funding Rate

FIL/USDT perpetual futures on Orderly Network operate through a shared order book infrastructure that aggregates liquidity across multiple DEX frontends. How Funding Works on Orderly Network

Orderly Network calculates FIL/USDT funding rates hourly based on the premium between perpetual and spot prices across its shared liquidity layer. The funding payment occurs at the top of each hour, with long positions paying shorts when rates are positive. Since Orderly operates as an infrastructure layer, funding calculations incorporate price feeds from multiple connected frontends, creating more stable rate discovery than isolated DEX platforms.

Why FIL/USDT?

FIL exhibits significant funding rate volatility due to its correlation with decentralized storage demand and periodic network upgrades. The token's infrastructure focus creates distinct trading patterns compared to pure DeFi tokens, offering unique arbitrage windows. Orderly's shared liquidity model provides deeper order books for FIL than typical DEX environments, enabling larger arbitrage positions.

Arbitrage Opportunities and Execution

Orderly's shared liquidity infrastructure enables arbitrage between funding rates and spot-perpetual basis across multiple connected DEX frontends simultaneously.
