Orderly Network INJ/USDT Funding Rate

How Funding Works on Orderly Network

Funding rates on Orderly Network settle every hour based on the premium/discount between INJ perpetual and spot prices across its shared order book. The network aggregates liquidity from various frontend partners, creating deeper markets that can maintain more stable funding rates compared to isolated DEX pools. Traders access these markets through integrated frontends while Orderly handles the underlying settlement and risk management across NEAR and EVM chains.

Cross-Chain Arbitrage Opportunities

Risk Management Considerations
