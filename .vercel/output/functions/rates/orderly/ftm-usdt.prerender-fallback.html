Orderly Network FTM/USDT Funding Rate

FTM/USDT perpetual futures on Orderly Network offer unique funding rate arbitrage opportunities through their shared liquidity infrastructure. How Funding Works on Orderly Network

Orderly Network calculates funding rates hourly using a time-weighted average price mechanism across its shared order book. The platform's infrastructure layer distributes funding settlements through smart contracts on NEAR, with rates typically ranging from -0.1% to 0.1% per hour. Frontend partners like WOOFi and Ref Finance access the same underlying liquidity, creating unified funding rate calculations despite multiple access points.

Why FTM/USDT?

FTM/USDT presents compelling arbitrage opportunities due to Fantom's position as a major L1 blockchain with active DeFi ecosystems. The pair often exhibits funding rate discrepancies between Orderly's DEX environment and centralized exchanges, particularly during periods of high on-chain activity or cross-chain bridge utilization.

FTM funding rates on Orderly Network have shown distinct patterns correlating with Fantom ecosystem developments and broader L1 blockchain narratives. Cross-DEX Arbitrage Opportunities

Orderly's unique position as an infrastructure layer creates arbitrage opportunities between its rates and other DEX protocols. Risk Management Considerations

Trading FTM perpetuals on Orderly Network involves specific risks related to its infrastructure dependencies and multi-chain architecture.
