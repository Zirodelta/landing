How Funding Works on RabbitX

RabbitX calculates funding rates hourly using a mark price derived from multiple spot exchanges and the perpetual's premium to spot. The funding exchange occurs at the top of each hour, with longs paying shorts when rates are positive. Starknet's validity proofs ensure settlement integrity while off-chain matching provides sub-second execution. Cross-margin positions automatically use available collateral to maintain funding payments without manual intervention.
