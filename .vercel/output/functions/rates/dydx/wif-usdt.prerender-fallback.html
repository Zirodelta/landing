How Funding Works on dYdX

dYdX calculates funding rates every hour based on the premium between perpetual and index prices, with payments exchanged between long and short position holders. The decentralized architecture ensures transparent rate calculations through on-chain oracle feeds and automated settlement. Funding rates are capped at ±0.75% per hour to prevent extreme scenarios, with typical rates ranging from -0.01% to +0.05% during normal market conditions.
