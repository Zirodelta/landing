How Funding Works on dYdX

dYdX v4 calculates SEI/USDT funding rates hourly using a time-weighted average premium between the perpetual contract price and SEI spot price across multiple exchanges. The funding rate is capped at ±1.875% per hour to prevent extreme rates during high volatility. Payments occur automatically at the top of each hour, with longs paying shorts when rates are positive and vice versa when negative. The platform's Cosmos appchain architecture enables more efficient rate calculations and settlements compared to Ethereum-based competitors.
