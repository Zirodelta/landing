How Funding Works on Hyperliquid

Hyperliquid calculates funding rates hourly based on the premium between perpetual and spot prices, with payments occurring every hour at the top of each hour. The on-chain order book ensures transparent price discovery without relying on external oracles for funding calculations. Funding rates are determined by the 1-hour TWAP of the premium, creating more frequent arbitrage opportunities compared to traditional 8-hour cycles.
