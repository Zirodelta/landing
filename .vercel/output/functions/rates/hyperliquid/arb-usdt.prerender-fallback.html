Hyperliquid ARB/USDT Funding Rate

ARB/USDT perpetual futures on Hyperliquid offer unique funding rate arbitrage opportunities through the platform's fully on-chain order book and 1-hour funding intervals. How Funding Works on Hyperliquid

Hyperliquid calculates funding rates every hour based on the premium/discount between the perpetual price and the underlying ARB spot price. The funding rate is determined by the 8-hour TWAP of the premium, capped at ±0.75% per funding period. Payments occur automatically at the top of each hour, with long positions paying short positions when the rate is positive. The on-chain settlement ensures transparent and tamper-proof funding rate calculations.
