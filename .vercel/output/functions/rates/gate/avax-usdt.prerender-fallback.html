Gate.io AVAX/USDT Funding Rate

Gate.io's AVAX/USDT perpetual futures contract follows an 8-hour funding rate cycle, settled at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC. As one of Gate.io's high-volume Layer 1 pairs, AVAX/USDT typically maintains stable funding rates due to strong liquidity depth.

How Funding Works on Gate.io

Gate.io calculates AVAX/USDT funding rates using a time-weighted average of the premium index over the 8-hour period. The exchange caps funding rates at ±0.375% per cycle to prevent extreme market distortions. Funding fees are exchanged directly between long and short position holders, with Gate.io collecting no additional fees on funding transfers. Position holders must maintain their positions at the funding timestamp to pay or receive funding.
