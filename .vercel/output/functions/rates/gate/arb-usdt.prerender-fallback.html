Gate.io ARB/USDT Funding Rate

ARB/USDT perpetual futures on Gate.io offer funding rate arbitrage opportunities with 8-hour settlement intervals. Gate.io's extensive altcoin coverage includes strong liquidity for Arbitrum's native token, making it suitable for medium to large position sizes.

How Funding Works on Gate.io

Gate.io calculates ARB/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using a time-weighted average premium index. Long positions pay when funding is positive, shorts pay when negative, with rates typically ranging from -0.5% to +2% during volatile periods. The exchange applies funding directly to unrealized PnL without affecting margin requirements, and positions held less than one funding period are exempt from the current interval's fee.

Arbitrage Opportunities and Strategy

Gate.io's 8-hour funding intervals create three daily arbitrage windows, with the 16:00 UTC settlement often showing the highest rate volatility due to Asian market overlap. Cross-exchange arbitrage opportunities frequently emerge between Gate.io and Binance, particularly when Gate.io's rates exceed 0.2% while competitors remain below 0.1%.
