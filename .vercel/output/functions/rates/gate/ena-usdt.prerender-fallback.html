How Funding Works on Gate.io

Gate.io calculates funding rates using a time-weighted average of the premium between ENA perpetual and spot prices over each 8-hour period. The rate is determined 15 minutes before each funding timestamp and applied to open positions. Long positions pay funding when rates are positive, while short positions pay when rates are negative. Gate.io caps funding rates at ±0.75% per 8-hour period to prevent extreme scenarios during high volatility.
