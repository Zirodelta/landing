How Funding Works on MEXC

MEXC calculates ORDI/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC based on the premium between perpetual and spot prices. Long positions pay funding when rates are positive, while shorts receive payments when rates turn negative. The exchange applies a funding fee cap of ±0.75% per interval to prevent extreme rate spikes during high volatility periods.
