How Funding Works on Vertex

Vertex calculates funding rates every hour using a mark price derived from external oracle feeds and internal orderbook dynamics. The hybrid sequencer model enables rapid price updates while maintaining on-chain settlement integrity. Cross-margin is enabled by default, allowing efficient capital utilization across multiple positions. Funding payments occur precisely at each hour mark, with rates typically ranging from -0.5% to +1.5% daily for PYTH during high volatility periods.
