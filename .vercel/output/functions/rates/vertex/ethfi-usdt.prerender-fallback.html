Vertex ETHFI/USDT Funding Rate

Vertex offers ETHFI/USDT perpetual futures with hourly funding rate payments, leveraging its hybrid DEX architecture on Arbitrum. The platform combines on-chain risk management with off-chain sequencer speed for efficient perpetual trading.

How Funding Works on Vertex

Vertex calculates funding rates hourly based on the premium between perpetual and spot prices. Payments occur every hour at the top of each hour, with rates determined by the 1-hour TWAP of the premium index. The hybrid architecture ensures accurate price feeds while maintaining low latency execution through the off-chain sequencer. Cross-margin is enabled by default, allowing efficient capital utilization across positions.
