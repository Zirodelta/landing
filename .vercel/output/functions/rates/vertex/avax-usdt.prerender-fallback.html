Vertex AVAX/USDT Funding Rate

AVAX/USDT perpetual futures on Vertex DEX offer hourly funding rate settlements with exceptionally low 0.02% taker fees. Vertex's hybrid architecture combines on-chain risk management with off-chain sequencer speed, making it efficient for high-frequency funding rate arbitrage strategies.

How Funding Works on Vertex

Vertex calculates funding rates hourly based on the 8-hour TWAP difference between perpetual and spot prices across multiple exchanges. The rate is applied automatically to all open positions at settlement, with long positions paying positive rates and receiving negative rates. Vertex's cross-margin system allows efficient capital utilization across multiple positions without requiring separate margin for each trade. The platform's sequencer processes funding settlements with minimal latency while maintaining on-chain risk controls.

Weekend trading typically sees compressed funding rate ranges due to lower institutional activity on Arbitrum.
