Vertex FTM/USDT Funding Rate

Vertex Protocol offers FTM/USDT perpetual futures with 1-hour funding intervals and cross-margin capabilities on Arbitrum. As a hybrid DEX combining on-chain risk management with off-chain sequencer speed, Vertex provides competitive 0.02%/0.03% trading fees for funding rate arbitrage strategies.

How Funding Works on Vertex

Vertex calculates funding rates hourly based on the premium between perpetual and spot prices, with payments exchanged between long and short positions. The protocol's on-chain risk engine ensures transparent rate calculations while the off-chain sequencer enables near-instant order execution. Cross-margin is enabled by default, allowing traders to use their entire account balance as collateral across all positions. Settlement occurs automatically through Vertex's smart contracts on Arbitrum.
