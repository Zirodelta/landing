How Funding Works on Binance

Binance calculates JTO/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using the premium index between perpetual and mark prices. The funding rate is capped at ±0.75% per interval, with adjustments based on the 8-hour TWAP of the premium. Positions held at funding time automatically exchange payments, with longs paying shorts when rates are positive. Binance's insurance fund provides additional protection against adverse funding rate scenarios.
