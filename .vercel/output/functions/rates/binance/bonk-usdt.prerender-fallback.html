Binance BONK/USDT Funding Rate

BONK/USDT perpetual futures on Binance offer funding rate arbitrage opportunities through the platform's 8-hour funding cycles. As the world's largest crypto exchange, Binance provides deep liquidity for this Solana-based meme coin, with funding rates calculated using the premium index between perpetual and spot prices.

How Funding Works on Binance

Binance calculates BONK/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using a time-weighted average of the premium index. The funding rate is capped at ±2% per funding interval to prevent extreme payments. When the perpetual price trades above spot (positive funding), long positions pay shorts; when below spot (negative funding), shorts pay longs. Payments are automatically debited or credited to account balances without requiring manual intervention.
