How Funding Works on Binance

Binance calculates W/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using a time-weighted average of the premium index over the funding interval. The rate is determined by the difference between the perpetual contract price and the underlying spot index, with a dampening mechanism to prevent extreme rates. Funding payments occur automatically between long and short position holders, with no fees charged by Binance on the transfer itself.
