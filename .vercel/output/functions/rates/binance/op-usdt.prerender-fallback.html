How Funding Works on Binance

Binance calculates OP/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using a time-weighted average premium over the final hour before settlement. The funding rate is capped at ±0.75% per 8-hour period, with most OP settlements ranging between -0.01% to +0.05%. Long positions pay funding when rates are positive, while short positions receive payments, creating systematic arbitrage opportunities during sustained directional moves.
