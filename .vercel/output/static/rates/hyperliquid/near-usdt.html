How Funding Works on Hyperliquid

Hyperliquid calculates NEAR/USDT funding rates every hour using a weighted average of the premium between perpetual and spot prices. The fully on-chain order book ensures transparent rate determination without centralized manipulation. Funding payments occur at the top of each hour, with rates typically ranging from -0.01% to 0.01% per hour. The L1 DEX architecture means all funding calculations are verifiable on-chain, providing transparency not available on centralized exchanges.
