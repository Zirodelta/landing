Hyperliquid RUNE/USDT Funding Rate

RUNE/USDT perpetual futures on Hyperliquid offer unique funding rate opportunities with hourly adjustments on a fully on-chain order book. As THORChain's native token, RUNE exhibits distinct funding patterns influenced by cross-chain liquidity dynamics and DeFi yield fluctuations.

How Funding Works on Hyperliquid

Hyperliquid calculates RUNE/USDT funding rates every hour using the premium/discount between the perpetual price and mark price derived from external exchanges. The funding rate is paid between long and short positions at each hourly interval, with rates typically ranging from -0.01% to +0.02% per hour. Settlement occurs automatically through the protocol's native USDC collateral system, eliminating counterparty risk while maintaining sub-second execution speeds.
