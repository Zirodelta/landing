Hyperliquid SEI/USDT Funding Rate

SEI/USDT perpetual futures on Hyperliquid offer unique funding rate opportunities through the platform's fully on-chain order book and 1-hour funding intervals. As a Layer-1 blockchain project, SEI's perpetual contract attracts both retail and institutional flow on this high-performance DEX.

How Funding Works on Hyperliquid

Hyperliquid calculates funding rates every hour based on the 1-hour TWAP premium between the perpetual price and the underlying index. The on-chain nature means all funding rate calculations are transparent and verifiable on Hyperliquid's L1 chain. Funding payments are automatically settled in USDC every hour at the top of the hour, with rates typically ranging from -0.01% to +0.01% per hour.

Arbitrage Opportunities

The on-chain nature of Hyperliquid creates unique arbitrage opportunities when funding rates diverge significantly from CEX platforms. Risk Considerations

USDC-only settlements mean exposure to USDC depeg risk, though this is mitigated by the stablecoin's track record. Liquidity can vary significantly during volatile periods, potentially impacting large position exits.
