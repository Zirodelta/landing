Orderly Network AAVE/USDT Funding Rate

AAVE/USDT perpetual futures on Orderly Network offer unique funding rate arbitrage opportunities through the platform's shared liquidity layer across multiple DEX frontends. Orderly's hourly funding rate calculations and cross-chain infrastructure create distinct rate patterns compared to centralized exchanges.

How Funding Works on Orderly Network

Orderly Network calculates AAVE/USDT funding rates every hour based on the premium between perpetual and spot prices across its shared order book. The rate is distributed across multiple DEX frontends including WOOFi Pro and Ref Finance, creating unified funding mechanics. Traders pay or receive funding based on their position direction and the 8-hour time-weighted average premium, with settlements occurring automatically through Orderly's infrastructure layer.

Why AAVE/USDT?

Cross-DEX Arbitrage Opportunities

Orderly's multi-frontend architecture enables arbitrage between different DEX interfaces accessing the same underlying liquidity. Rate discrepancies of 0.02-0.05% frequently occur between WOOFi Pro and Ref Finance due to varying user bases and trading patterns. The NEAR/EVM bridge integration creates additional arbitrage potential when Ethereum-based AAVE perpetuals diverge from Orderly rates. Traders can exploit these inefficiencies while benefiting from the shared order book's improved liquidity depth.

Risk Management Considerations

Lower trading volumes compared to major CEX platforms may result in wider spreads during volatile periods, affecting arbitrage profitability.
