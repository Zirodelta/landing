How Funding Works on Orderly Network

The funding exchange occurs between long and short position holders based on the difference between the perpetual price and the underlying spot price. Orderly's infrastructure layer distributes this funding calculation across all connected frontend partners, ensuring consistent rates regardless of which DEX interface traders use.
