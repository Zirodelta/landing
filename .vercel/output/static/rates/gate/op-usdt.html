Gate.io OP/USDT Funding Rate

How Funding Works on Gate.io

Gate.io calculates funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC based on the premium between perpetual and spot prices. Long positions pay short positions when funding is positive, reflecting bullish sentiment and spot premium. The exchange uses a dampening mechanism to prevent extreme rates, with caps typically around ±2% per funding period. Funding payments are automatically debited or credited to your futures account balance without affecting your position size.
