GMX BONK/USDT Funding Rate

BONK/USDT perpetual futures on GMX offer unique arbitrage opportunities through the platform's oracle-based pricing and hourly funding mechanism. As a leading Solana meme token, BONK exhibits volatile funding patterns driven by retail sentiment and cross-chain trading dynamics.

How Funding Works on GMX

GMX calculates BONK/USDT funding rates hourly based on the difference between perpetual price and oracle spot price from Chainlink feeds. Traders pay or receive funding every hour at the top of each hour, with rates typically ranging from -0.01% to +0.05% during normal market conditions. The oracle dependency means funding rates may diverge from order book exchanges, creating arbitrage windows when BONK experiences rapid price movements or when Solana network congestion affects cross-chain price discovery.

Arbitrage Opportunities and Execution

The primary arbitrage strategy involves exploiting funding rate differentials between GMX and centralized exchanges like Binance or OKX. When GMX funding turns positive due to long bias, traders can short BONK/USDT on GMX while longing on CEXs to capture the spread.
