GMX STX/USDT Funding Rate

How Funding Works on GMX

GMX calculates STX funding rates every hour based on the premium/discount between the perpetual price and Chainlink oracle feeds. The funding mechanism uses GLP pool liquidity to maintain price stability, with rates typically ranging from -0.01% to 0.01% per hour. When STX perpetuals trade at a premium to spot, long positions pay shorts, creating opportunities for delta-neutral strategies. The oracle dependency means funding rates respond more gradually to rapid STX price movements compared to order book exchanges.
