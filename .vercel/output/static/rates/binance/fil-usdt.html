Binance FIL/USDT Funding Rate

How Funding Works on Binance

Binance calculates FIL/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using the premium/discount between perpetual price and mark price. Long positions pay short positions when funding is positive, reflecting bullish sentiment. The exchange applies a dampening factor to prevent extreme rates, typically capping at ±2.5% per funding period. Settlement occurs automatically in USDT margin accounts.
