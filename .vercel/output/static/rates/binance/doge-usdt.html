How Funding Works on Binance

Binance calculates DOGE/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using a weighted average of the premium/discount between perpetual and mark price. The funding fee is exchanged directly between long and short position holders, with no fees going to Binance. Maximum funding rate is capped at ±2.5% per interval, though DOGE rarely reaches these extremes. Position holders only pay funding if holding positions during settlement times.
