How Funding Works on Binance

Binance calculates MATIC/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using a time-weighted average of the premium between perpetual and mark prices. The funding fee is exchanged directly between long and short position holders, with Binance taking no cut from the funding mechanism. Position holders only pay or receive funding if they hold positions at the exact funding timestamp, allowing for strategic entry and exit timing.
