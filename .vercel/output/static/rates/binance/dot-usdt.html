How Funding Works on Binance

Binance calculates DOT/USDT funding rates using a time-weighted average of the premium between perpetual and spot prices over the 8-hour period. The funding fee is exchanged between long and short position holders at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC, with rates typically ranging from -0.375% to +0.375% annually. Binance's insurance fund covers any funding payment shortfalls, ensuring consistent settlement even during extreme market conditions with DOT's parachain auction volatility.
