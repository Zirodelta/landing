How Funding Works on Bitget

Bitget calculates ARB/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using a time-weighted average of the premium between perpetual and spot prices. The exchange applies a dampening mechanism to reduce extreme rate volatility, typically capping rates at ±0.75% per interval. Long positions pay funding when rates are positive, while short positions receive payments, with settlements occurring automatically without affecting available margin.
