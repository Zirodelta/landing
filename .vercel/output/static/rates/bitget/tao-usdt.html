How Funding Works on Bitget

Bitget calculates TAO/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using a time-weighted average of the premium/discount between perpetual and spot prices. The funding rate is capped at ±0.75% per interval to prevent extreme payments. Long positions pay when rates are positive, while shorts pay when negative, with settlements occurring automatically without affecting margin balances until the next funding window.
