How Funding Works on Phemex

Phemex calculates NEAR/USDT funding rates using a weighted average of the premium between perpetual and mark prices over the previous 8 hours. Funding exchanges occur at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC, with rates typically ranging from -0.01% to 0.375% per interval. Long positions pay funding when rates are positive, while shorts receive payments when rates turn negative.
