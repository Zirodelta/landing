How Funding Works on Bybit

Bybit calculates ENA/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC based on the premium between perpetual and spot prices. The funding fee equals position size × funding rate × 1, paid between long and short holders. Bybit's unified trading account allows seamless hedging across spot and derivatives markets. The exchange uses a time-weighted average of the premium over the 8-hour period, reducing manipulation risk compared to shorter intervals.
