How Funding Works on Paradex

Paradex calculates NEAR/USDT funding rates using a time-weighted average premium between perpetual and spot prices over the hour. Funding settlements occur every hour at the top of each hour, with rates paid between long and short holders. The exchange uses a robust oracle system and applies a dampening factor to prevent excessive rate volatility during high market stress.
