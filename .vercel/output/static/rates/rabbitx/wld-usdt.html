How Funding Works on RabbitX

RabbitX calculates funding rates every hour based on the premium between perpetual and mark prices, with payments exchanged between long and short positions. The platform's Starknet-based settlement ensures instant execution without gas costs, while off-chain order matching provides minimal slippage. Cross-margin capabilities allow efficient capital allocation across multiple positions. Funding payments are automatically debited or credited to trader balances at each hourly interval.
