How Funding Works on MEXC

MEXC calculates NEAR/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC, based on the premium between perpetual and spot prices. Long positions pay funding when rates are positive, while short positions receive payments. The zero maker fee structure on MEXC significantly reduces arbitrage costs, as traders can enter spot positions without paying maker fees when hedging perpetual exposure.
