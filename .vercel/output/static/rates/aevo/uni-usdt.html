Aevo UNI/USDT Funding Rate

UNI/USDT perpetual futures on Aevo offer unique funding rate arbitrage opportunities through hourly settlements and the platform's specialized rollup infrastructure. As Uniswap's governance token, UNI exhibits distinct volatility patterns that create predictable funding rate cycles, particularly around protocol governance events and DeFi market movements.

How Funding Works on Aevo

Aevo calculates funding rates hourly using a time-weighted average of the premium between perpetual and mark prices over the settlement window. The platform's rollup architecture enables efficient on-chain settlement with reduced gas costs compared to mainnet DEXs. Funding payments occur automatically at each hour mark, with rates typically ranging from -0.05% to +0.15% for UNI/USDT during normal market conditions. The exchange's off-chain order book provides real-time rate predictions while maintaining decentralized settlement.

Why UNI/USDT?

UNI/USDT funding rates on Aevo often diverge from centralized exchanges due to the platform's DeFi-focused user base and governance token speculation cycles. Risk Considerations

The platform's smaller liquidity pool means large funding rate movements can occur with relatively modest position sizes, requiring careful position management. Rollup bridge risks also apply to USDT collateral, though Aevo's institutional backing provides some security assurances compared to newer rollup DEXs.
