How Funding Works on BingX

BingX calculates FTM/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC using the premium/discount mechanism. The funding rate is determined by the difference between the perpetual contract price and the spot index, with a dampening factor applied during high volatility. Long positions pay short positions when the rate is positive, with payments automatically settled from account balances. BingX caps extreme funding rates at ±2% per cycle to prevent excessive costs during market stress.
