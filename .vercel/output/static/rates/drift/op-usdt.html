Drift OP/USDT Funding Rate

OP/USDT perpetual futures on Drift offer unique arbitrage opportunities through the protocol's 1-hour funding rate cycles and hybrid vAMM-DLOB architecture. As Optimism's native token, OP exhibits distinct funding patterns driven by L2 adoption cycles and ecosystem developments. How Funding Works on Drift

Drift calculates OP/USDT funding rates every hour using a time-weighted average price (TWAP) between perpetual and mark prices over the funding period. The vAMM component provides continuous liquidity while the DLOB (Decentralized Limit Order Book) enables precise order execution during funding settlements. JIT (Just-in-Time) liquidity auctions activate during high volatility periods, potentially creating additional funding rate deviations. Insurance fund contributions help stabilize rates during extreme market conditions.
