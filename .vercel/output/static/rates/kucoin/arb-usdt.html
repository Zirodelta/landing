How Funding Works on KuCoin

KuCoin calculates funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC for ARB/USDT perpetuals. The funding rate equals (Premium Index + clamp(Interest Rate - Premium Index, -0.05%, 0.05%)) where Premium Index reflects the perpetual's premium to spot. Positive rates require long positions to pay shorts, while negative rates reverse this flow. KuCoin's funding calculations use a time-weighted average price during the final hour before settlement.
