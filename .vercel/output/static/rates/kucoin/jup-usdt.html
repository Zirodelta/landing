How Funding Works on KuCoin

KuCoin calculates JUP/USDT funding rates every 8 hours at 00:00, 08:00, and 16:00 UTC based on the premium between perpetual and index prices. The funding rate is capped at ±0.75% per interval to prevent extreme payments. Long positions pay short positions when the rate is positive, reflecting bullish sentiment and futures premium. KuCoin's funding mechanism uses a time-weighted average of the premium index over the 8-hour period to smooth out short-term volatility.

Historical Funding Rate Patterns
