OKX vs Bitget

Side-by-side comparison of funding rate mechanics, fees, and perpetual trading on OKX and Bitget.

Verdict

OKX offers superior infrastructure for serious arbitrageurs with its portfolio margin and robust API, making it better suited for sophisticated strategies. Bitget provides more aggressive rate movements and faster new token listings, appealing to traders seeking higher volatility opportunities despite less mature tooling.

Rate Mechanics

Both exchanges calculate funding rates every 8 hours using similar mark-to-index methodologies. OKX uses a weighted average of multiple index sources with tighter spreads during volatile periods. Bitget's rates tend to be more responsive to immediate market sentiment but can experience higher volatility. OKX's portfolio margin system allows for more efficient capital utilization when arbitraging across multiple pairs simultaneously.
