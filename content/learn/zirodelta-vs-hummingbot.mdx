## The Fundamental Difference

**Hummingbot and Zirodelta solve different problems for different users.**

**Hummingbot** is a full-featured algorithmic trading framework that supports dozens of strategies across hundreds of venues. It's open source, self-hosted, and infinitely customizable - but requires significant technical expertise and operational overhead.

**Zirodelta** is a specialized funding rate arbitrage platform that automates cross-exchange yield capture through a simple Telegram interface. It does one thing exceptionally well rather than many things adequately.

Based on our analysis of 9.4M+ funding rate settlements across 4 exchanges (2019-2026), we've gained unique insights into specialized versus general-purpose arbitrage approaches.

**The choice comes down to:** Do you want maximum flexibility with high complexity, or optimized execution for a specific high-yield strategy?
