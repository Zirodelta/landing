## Bybit-Specific Platform Mechanics

**Arbitrage benefits:**

**Bybit formula (standard but different index sources):**
```
Funding Rate = Premium Index + clamp(Interest Rate - Premium Index, -0.05%, 0.05%)
```

**Index price sources:** Coinbase Pro, Bitstamp, Kraken (excludes Binance)
