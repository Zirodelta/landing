# Best Funding Rate Bots in 2026 - Complete Ranking

The funding rate arbitrage market has matured significantly. In 2026, you have more options than ever - from exchange-native bots to independent tools to Telegram-based execution. But they're not all equal.

We've tested and analyzed every major funding rate tool available. Here's our honest ranking based on cost, exchange coverage, execution capabilities, and real-world usability.

## What Makes a Good Funding Rate Bot?

Before ranking, here's what matters:

- **Cross-exchange support** - Rate divergence between exchanges creates the best opportunities. Single-exchange bots miss this entirely.
- **Execution vs alerts** - Screeners show you rates. Execution bots actually open the hedge. Big difference when a 0.5% rate appears at 3 AM.
- **Cost** - Some tools charge $50-200/month. For small accounts, fees can eat the entire edge.
- **Data depth** - Better historical data = better opportunity detection. Our dataset spans 9.4M+ settlements across 6+ years.
- **Delta-neutral execution** - The bot should open both legs (spot buy + perp short) simultaneously to minimize exposure.
